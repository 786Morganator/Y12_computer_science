About Us

Yassin Horizons is a newly established organization dedicated to supporting Malaysian students in Perth, Australia. Founded in 2023 by Mrs Yassin, we are in our nascent form but driven by a strong vision to create a bridge between cultures and foster academic excellence. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support to Malaysian students throughout their educational journey in Perth.

Our Story

Yassin Horizons was born from the personal experiences and passion of our founder, Mrs Yassin. Recognizing the challenges faced by Malaysian students studying abroad, Mrs envisioned a support system that could make their transition and stay in Perth smoother and more enriching. Though we are just beginning our journey, we are committed to growing into a robust support network for the Malaysian student community in Perth.

Our Team

As a new organization, our team is small but dedicated. Led by Mrs Yassin, we are actively recruiting experienced educators, cultural ambassadors, and student mentors who share our vision. We are committed to building a diverse team that can address the unique needs of Malaysian students in Australia.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering students to reach their full potential.
  • Inclusivity: We are building an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our programs and services.
  • Community: We aim to build strong, supportive communities that last beyond university years.

Our Vision for Impact

While we are just starting out, our goal is to make a significant impact on the lives of Malaysian students in Perth. We envision supporting hundreds of students in the coming years, helping them navigate their academic journey, cultural transition, and personal growth. We look forward to seeing our future alumni succeed in their careers and give back to our growing community.

Join Our Growing Community

As a new organization, we are eager to welcome students, mentors, and community members who are interested in our cause. Whether you're a student seeking support, a professional looking to mentor, or a community member interested in contributing to our growth, we invite you to be part of the Yassin Horizons family from its early stages.

Our Services

At Yassin Horizons, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support Malaysian students throughout their academic journey in Perth, Australia. Our goal is to ensure that every student has the resources and support they need to thrive both academically and personally.

Cultural Adaptation Support

We understand that adjusting to a new culture can be challenging. Our cultural adaptation support services include:

  • Pre-departure orientation sessions
  • Welcome programs upon arrival in Perth
  • Cultural awareness workshops
  • One-on-one cultural mentoring

Academic Excellence Program

Our academic support services are designed to help you achieve your full potential:

  • Study skills workshops
  • Subject-specific tutoring
  • Academic writing support
  • Exam preparation assistance

Career Development

We help prepare you for your future career:

  • Resume and cover letter writing workshops
  • Interview skills training
  • Networking events with local businesses
  • Internship placement assistance

Social Integration

Building a supportive community is crucial for your success and well-being:

  • Regular social events and outings
  • Cultural exchange programs
  • Volunteer opportunities in the local community
  • Sports and recreational activities

Mental Health and Wellness

We prioritize your mental health and overall well-being:

  • Counseling services
  • Stress management workshops
  • Mindfulness and meditation sessions
  • Access to mental health resources

Our services are designed to be comprehensive and adaptable to your individual needs. We're committed to supporting you every step of the way during your time in Perth.
